How We Work

A simple, transparent, and safe food-sharing process.

1. Donor uploads surplus

Restaurants list leftover meals with quantity and pickup time.

2. Volunteers get notified

Nearby volunteers receive alerts instantly.

3. Food pickup & verification

Hygiene checklist ensures safe and fresh transfer.

4. Delivery to shelters

Meals reach NGOs, shelters, and communities in need.
